Venues

The conference will be held at two venues in beautiful Montréal Québec. On Tuesday, Thursday and Friday events will take place at the Schulich School of Music at McGill University and on Wednesday the conference will spend the day across town at the Faculty of Music at the Université de Montréal.


McGill University
Location Wirth Music Building
Address 527 Sherbrooke St. West
Website mcgill.ca/music
Subway access McGill Station, Green Line
Bus stop Bus 24 stops in front of the building.

The main location for conference activities at McGill is Tanna Schulich Hall located on the second floor. The main lobby (Wirth Lobby) is where lunch and refreshments are served and where the poster sessions will be held. Some sessions will be held in other nearby rooms. Signs will be posted, and you can get directions at the Welcome Desk in the lobby.


Université de Montréal
Location Pavillon de la Faculte de Musique
Address 200 Av. Vincent-D’Indy
Website musique.umontreal.ca
Subway access Édouard-Monpetit, Blue Line
Bus stop Bus 51 stops on Blvd. Édouard-Monpetit.

The conference centre at the Université de Montréal is Salle Claude-Champagne a beautiful performance venue that is also fitted with state of the art research equipment. The foyer of Salle Claude-Champagne serves as the break area and will host apertifs and dinner before the concert Wednesday night. Rooms in other areas of the faculty of Music will used for the conference activities. Signs will be posted and a staffed Welcome Desk can assist with directions.
