    Local Poster Printing Service

    The poster panels fit A0 in portrait orientation (i.e., “vertical”). We have partnered with a local printing service to offer the option of having the posters printed on site at the cost of 24€ per A0 poster. Please note that this is a third-party service provider not affiliated with the conference organization. Authors of poster presentations are required to upload a pdf (with locked font and size) using the following link: https://eshop.galonis.gr/product/research-posters/ no later than 5 July 2023. The posters will be delivered at the venue to the presenters who chose to use the service.

    Attention! The deadline for submitting the pdfs to the printing service is the 5 July 2023 so that the posters will be printed on time. This is a hard deadline that will be enforced by the local printing service. Be aware that pdfs uploaded using the link above after the 5th of July of 2023 will not be printed and thus will not be available at the conference venue.
